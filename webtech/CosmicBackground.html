GigaMoon

Vishesh Kumar Special

GigaMoon shows us billions of years of universe-scale history written into the surface. Its awe-inspiring; solidified lava lakes, worm-like collapsed lava tubes, permanently shaded canyons and the patterns of impact craters that reveal the random brutality of the universe. The scale of GigaMoon is epic, my biggest moon image yet. It draws you in and holds you in awe.
